GNUKhata (http://gnukhata.org) is a free and open source accounting software. It is suitable both for small organisations and for enterprises.
In this comprehensive series of spoken tutorials, we will cover all aspects of accounting under GNUKhata.

Having created an organisation, we choose to create accounts.

Now you see a number of textboxes.

The first is Group name. Here you click on dropdown you get to see all Group names provided by the software. All you have to do is to select a group name. Here I

choose Current Asset as Group name.

After selecting Group name you have to select subgroup name. These subgroups are also provided by the software. I choose bank as a sud group name. Here you have an

option to create a new subgroup as per your requirement.

When you select create new subgroup option a new textbox appears on the screen. Here you will enter name of the subgroup which you want to create.

Next filed is Account name, here I enter the name of the Account name called salary.

Of the main groups sum will have opening balances, while incomes and expenses will not have opening balance.

for groups like capital, current liability, loans(liability), the accounts will have opening credit balance and for groups like current assets fixed assets , loans
(assest) the accounts will have opening debite balance.

As the name suggest Total credit opening balance gives you the total of all credit opening balances so far entered.

similarly Total debit opening balance gives you the total of all debit opening balances so far entered.

Difference in opening balance gives differance between debit and credit opening balances.

If all the opening balances correctly entered,this will be zero.

While entering opening debit or credit balance you can enter zero, press the enter key, enter any positive or negative amount. I enter 5000.

If you enter a negative amount in the opening balance, it will change the type of opening balance from debit to credit or vice versa.

After filling all the data I press save button.

Here you are, message appears at the top. It tells you have successfully created the desired account.

If you do not desire to create any more accounts, then select Finish option.

Congratulations! you have now created the books for the organisation IITB.

There are a few basic house keeping tasks you will have to do now.

Let's first understand the analogy of a master and a transaction.

In master you have six options like Account creation, Customizable voucher and Bank Reconciliation .

In Transactions you have option to enter different types of transactions like Contra, Journal, Payment, Receipt, Credit Note, Debit Note,
Sales, Purchase, Sales
Return, Purchase Return and there is one more that is Other. etc.

In this you can define your own transaction.

Report option provides facility to display and print individual ledger accounts, trial balance, Balance sheet and profit and loss account
.
Administration option provides facilities for new user, change of password, take backup and restore database.
