After creating accounts, we come to recording of transactions.

Here you can enter different types of transactions such as Contra, Journal, Payment, Receipt, Credit Note, Debit Note, Sales, Purchase, Sales Return, Purchase Return
and there is one more that is Other.In this you can define your own transaction.

Let us take Contra voucher. The record of this voucher involves two accounts from Current Assets, Sub Group Cash and Bank.

The first text-box is for Reference Number. This is important because you can use this for finding a record in Find/Edit Voucher
.
This text-box can take alphanumeric characters.So I type CA12. Next comes the Date field, it is to be entered in DD-MM-YYYY manner. I type 01-02-2011.

For recording a voucher you have to fill the table appearing on the screen. This table contains four fields.

The first is Dr/Cr. Here you have a drop-down box with Dr/Cr options. Depending upon whether you want to debit or credit an account you will select DR or CR option.

I have selected Dr option.

Next field is Account Name. Here also you have a dropdown box. It shows created account names. I have selected Cash account.

The next is Debit Amount, here in the textbox you are supposed to enter amount. I have entered 2000. Then I pressed Ok button to indicate that this part of the record of the transaction is correct.

Now we see second part of the transaction that is credit side. Now I select Credit option from this dropdown. From the Account Name I select HDFC Bank.

Because I have selected Credit option from the first dropdown. The amount will be entered in credit column. I entered 2000. Then pressed Ok button.

At this point if you realise that there is a mistake in the data entered, you can Edit that row by pressing the Edit symbol which appears in that row.

When you do this you can re-enter the data. If you want to delete the row, you press the delete symbol.

If the data is entered correctly total of debit amounts and credit amounts will be equal.

Now we come to Narration box, here you can write whatever details of the transaction.
I type "Being cash withdrawn from bank". When you are done with this you can
press the Save button.

See what happens When I press Save button. Here I get to get record another transaction but at the top of this message shown "Record has been saved".

Now I will show you how to find a record. This can be done by entering reference number or by entering date of transaction.

I choose Reference no. So here I enter Reference No CA12. Now you will realise the importance of entering unique reference number.

Let us search a transaction by date of transaction. IF you are not certain about the date of transaction you can enter period in the form of From date - To date.

Here I enter 01-02-2011. For To date field I enter 28-02-2011.

See what happens When I press Search button. The programme has identify the transaction.

It has displayed the data of the transaction in the form of Reference No, Voucher type, Date of transaction and Narration.

If you want to see details of the transaction you can press the View button. When I press the view button the entire transaction is displayed on the screen.

Below this you will notice three buttons, namely, save to file, Edit and clone.

When I press save to file button. Here you are, the programme generates a PDF file of the record. As you know a PDF file can be printed.
